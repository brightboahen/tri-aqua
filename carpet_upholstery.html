Carpet & Upholstery

The Dry Fusion way Commercial - Domestic

Dry fusion is recommended by all major carpet and carpet tile companies, beecause it cleans, Deodorizes, kills bacteria, and protects and has your carpet dry in 30 minutes and is recommended safe for children and a household pets

Although traditional carpet cleaning methods have their benefits, many are frustrated with the disadvantages that come with it including the length of time it takes to dry, shrinkage and the unpleasant odours that wet carpets give off the dry fusion low moisture system will eliminate these disadvantages.

Method of Cleaning

We vacuum your carpet with a twin motored vacuum. We then lightly spray your carpet with a citrus based chemical, we then extract the chemical and dirt with our dry fusion system allowing the chemical to bond to each fibre of your carpet, stopping fungus and microbes growing on the fibres of the carpet leaving your carpet clean revitalised and fresh and because the fungus and microbes and grit cannot bond to the fibres creating less dust.

Your staff will have fewer reactions from hay fever and fungal related allergies and because the grit cannot bond to your carpet if vacuumed on a regular basis, your carpets will last longer, because there is less grit cutting into the fibres of your carpet.
